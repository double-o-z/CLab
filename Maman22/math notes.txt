Multiply work like this: 
Each item in first row of first matrix is multiplied by the relevant item in the first column of the second matrix. 
The multiplications are summed and the value is the item in 0,0 of the result matrix.
Then each item in first row of first matrix is multiplied by the relevant item in the second column of the second matrix.
and so forth.
